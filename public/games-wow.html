World of Warcraft (WOW)

World of Warcraft is an Online Massive Multiplayer Online Role Playing Game (MMORPG) that features hundreds of hours of gameplay and multiple expansions each year. Released in 2004, it has an engaging and evolving storyline that is influenced by player interaction, a player vs player arena to show off your skills, and tons of areas to explore.
