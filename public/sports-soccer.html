Soccer

If your looking to join a team or just talk about the sport, this group is for you. We welcome soccer enthusiasts and newcomers alike to come join us and talk about the sport. There are for-fun games every week, just visit the Dixon Center or join the group for more info. We welcome people of all skill levels to join us for some fun. Our games are held at the fields across from the Dixon Center, so come on out and have a good time!
